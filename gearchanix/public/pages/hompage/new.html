Welcome To PUP Motor Pool
PUP Motor Pool manages all the vehicles used by PUP Main Campus and Branches.
Their primary concern is the maintenance of the PUP vehicles to keep them in working condition.

About Us

The PUP Motor Pool is the backbone of the university’s transportation management, ensuring a fleet of reliable and well-maintained vehicles is always ready to support daily operations, academic activities, and logistical needs.

With a skilled team of mechanics and technical staff, the Motor Pool has integrated the GEARCHANIX Maintenance Management System, shifting from manual methods to a digital solution, allowing for efficient scheduling, predictive maintenance, and real-time monitoring.

Dedicated to transparency, accountability, and superior service quality, the Motor Pool remains focused on enhancing the university’s transportation capabilities, meeting and exceeding the expectations of our academic community.
